Steps to Setup ScreenSharing: (Note: support from chrome only as of now)
    1). Download the chrome extension from Here
    2). Click the icon of the "WebRTC Desktop Sharing" extension from top right corner of Google Chrome.
    3). Navigate to options and tick the box "Room URL Box".
    4). Click the icon of the extension again and select any of the listed option to start hosting.
    5). From the pop-up window that appears copy the link and paste it as "Web link" down below So that others can join.
